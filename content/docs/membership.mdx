## Membership Overview

Joining SPMA connects you with a community of project management professionals dedicated to advancing the profession in Sudan. Members gain access to exclusive resources, events, and networking opportunities.

## Membership Types

### Professional Member
For certified project management practitioners holding PMP, CAPM, PMI-ACP, or equivalent certifications.

- Full voting rights in association matters
- Access to all SPMA events and workshops
- Discounted rates on training programs
- Mentorship opportunities
- Listing in the SPMA professional directory

### Associate Member
For individuals working in project management or related fields without formal certification.

- Access to SPMA events and workshops
- Discounted rates on training programs
- Certification preparation support
- Networking opportunities

### Student Member
For undergraduate and graduate students studying project management or related disciplines.

- Access to student-focused events
- Mentorship from professional members
- Discounted certification exam fees
- Career guidance and job placement support

### Corporate Member
For organizations seeking to develop their project management capabilities.

- Multiple employee memberships at reduced rates
- Customized in-house training programs
- Priority access to SPMA consulting services
- Brand visibility at SPMA events

## Requirements

| Membership Type | Education | Experience | Certification |
|----------------|-----------|------------|---------------|
| Professional | Bachelor's degree | 3+ years in PM | PMP or equivalent |
| Associate | Any level | Any PM experience | Not required |
| Student | Currently enrolled | Not required | Not required |
| Corporate | N/A | N/A | N/A |

## Benefits

All members enjoy:

- **Knowledge Resources** — Access to the SPMA digital library, templates, and best practice guides
- **Professional Network** — Connect with PM professionals across Sudan and the region
- **Career Growth** — Job postings, career workshops, and mentorship programs
- **Events** — Priority registration and discounts for conferences, workshops, and seminars
- **Recognition** — Professional development units (PDUs) for participation in SPMA activities

## How to Join

1. **Visit** the SPMA membership page
2. **Select** your membership type
3. **Complete** the application form with your professional details
4. **Submit** supporting documents (certificates, transcripts, or company registration)
5. **Pay** the annual membership fee
6. **Receive** your membership confirmation and welcome package
