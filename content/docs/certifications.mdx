## PMI Certification Pathways

SPMA supports members pursuing internationally recognized project management certifications from the Project Management Institute (PMI). Our programs include preparation courses, study groups, and mentorship.

### Project Management Professional (PMP)

The PMP certification is the gold standard for project managers worldwide. It demonstrates competence in leading and directing projects.

**Eligibility Requirements:**

- **Four-year degree:** 36 months leading projects + 35 hours PM education
- **High school diploma:** 60 months leading projects + 35 hours PM education

**Exam Structure:**

- 180 questions (multiple choice and multiple response)
- 230 minutes to complete
- Covers three domains: People (42%), Process (50%), Business Environment (8%)

### Certified Associate in Project Management (CAPM)

CAPM is designed for those starting their project management career. It demonstrates foundational knowledge of project management principles.

**Eligibility Requirements:**

- High school diploma or equivalent
- 23 hours of project management education

**Exam Structure:**

- 150 questions
- 180 minutes to complete

### PMI Agile Certified Practitioner (PMI-ACP)

PMI-ACP recognizes expertise in agile principles and practices across agile methodologies including Scrum, Kanban, Lean, XP, and TDD.

**Eligibility Requirements:**

- 2,000 hours working on project teams (within last 5 years)
- 1,500 hours working on agile project teams
- 21 hours of agile practices training

## SPMA Preparation Programs

### Study Groups

SPMA organizes weekly study groups for each certification level. Groups meet virtually and in-person, covering:

- PMBOK Guide chapters and knowledge areas
- Practice exams and question analysis
- Real-world scenario discussions
- Tips and strategies for exam day

### Workshops

Intensive preparation workshops are offered quarterly:

- **PMP Boot Camp** — 5-day intensive covering all exam domains
- **CAPM Essentials** — 3-day foundational program
- **Agile Intensive** — 3-day PMI-ACP preparation

### Mentorship Program

Certified SPMA members volunteer as mentors to guide candidates through:

- Study plan development and timeline
- Application process assistance
- Exam readiness assessment
- Post-certification PDU maintenance

## Continuing Education

PMI requires certified professionals to earn Professional Development Units (PDUs) to maintain their certifications. SPMA events, workshops, and study groups are registered as PDU-eligible activities.

**PDU Requirements:**

| Certification | PDUs Required | Cycle Length |
|--------------|--------------|-------------|
| PMP | 60 PDUs | 3 years |
| CAPM | 15 PDUs | 3 years |
| PMI-ACP | 30 PDUs | 3 years |
