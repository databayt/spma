## Annual Conference

The SPMA Annual Conference is the premier project management event in Sudan, bringing together practitioners, academics, and industry leaders to share knowledge and discuss the future of the profession.

### Conference Highlights

- **Keynote Speakers** — International and regional experts in project management
- **Technical Sessions** — Deep-dive presentations on PM methodologies, tools, and case studies
- **Workshops** — Hands-on sessions covering practical skills
- **Networking** — Structured networking sessions connecting professionals across industries
- **Exhibition** — Showcase of PM tools, services, and training providers

### Past Conference Themes

- **2025:** "Project Management in Crisis: Building Resilience"
- **2024:** "Digital Transformation and the Future of Projects"
- **2023:** "Sustainable Development through Effective Project Management"

## Monthly Seminars

SPMA hosts monthly seminars open to members and the public, covering current topics in project management.

**Format:**

- 2-hour evening sessions
- Expert speaker presentation (60 min)
- Q&A and discussion (30 min)
- Networking (30 min)

**Recent Topics:**

- Risk Management in Uncertain Environments
- Agile Adoption in Traditional Organizations
- Stakeholder Engagement Strategies
- Project Leadership vs. Project Management
- Earned Value Management in Practice

## Training Programs

### Professional Development Series

A structured series of courses designed to build competencies progressively:

1. **Fundamentals of Project Management** — For beginners and career changers
2. **Advanced Project Planning & Scheduling** — CPM, PERT, and resource leveling
3. **Risk Management Masterclass** — Qualitative and quantitative risk analysis
4. **Agile Project Management** — Scrum, Kanban, and hybrid approaches
5. **Project Leadership** — Soft skills, team dynamics, and conflict resolution

### Industry-Specific Programs

Tailored training for specific sectors:

- **Construction Project Management** — Covering procurement, contract management, and site supervision
- **IT Project Management** — Software development lifecycle, DevOps, and cloud migration projects
- **Oil & Gas Project Management** — Upstream and downstream project challenges
- **Healthcare Project Management** — Hospital construction, health system reform projects

## Community Programs

### Young Project Managers Forum

A quarterly forum for early-career professionals (under 30) featuring:

- Peer-to-peer learning sessions
- Career development workshops
- Mentorship matching
- Innovation challenges

### Women in Project Management

A dedicated program supporting women professionals through:

- Leadership development workshops
- Networking events
- Mentorship circles
- Annual awards for outstanding contributions

### University Outreach

SPMA partners with Sudanese universities to:

- Deliver guest lectures on project management
- Support PM curriculum development
- Offer student memberships at reduced rates
- Sponsor capstone projects and competitions
