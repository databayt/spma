## Templates & Tools

### Free Templates

SPMA provides members with downloadable templates for common project management activities:

- **Project Charter Template** — Define project scope, objectives, and stakeholders
- **Work Breakdown Structure (WBS)** — Hierarchical decomposition of project deliverables
- **Risk Register** — Track identified risks, probability, impact, and response plans
- **Stakeholder Register** — Document stakeholder interests, influence, and engagement strategy
- **Lessons Learned Template** — Capture project insights for future improvement
- **Status Report Template** — Weekly/monthly project status communication
